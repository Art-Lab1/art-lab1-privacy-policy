Art-Lab1 Privacy Policy

Company: Art-Lab1 • Last updated: 2025-10-21

This Privacy Policy applies to Art-Lab1, a personal tool used to upload images (pins) to Pinterest via the official Pinterest API.

Data We Process

  • Authorization data (Pinterest OAuth token, expiration time, board ID) — stored locally.
  • Content you choose to publish (images, titles, descriptions, links).
  • Optional local error logs (not shared with third parties).

How to Revoke Access / Delete Data

  • Revoke OAuth access in Pinterest developer settings or delete the local token.
  • Delete pins directly in Pinterest.
  • Remove local configuration and logs from your device.
