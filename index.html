Art-Lab1 Privacy Policy

Application: Art-Lab1 • Company: Art-Lab1 • Last updated: 2025-10-21

This Privacy Policy applies to Art-Lab1, a personal tool used by its owner to upload images (pins) to Pinterest via the official Pinterest API.

1. Who We Are

Art-Lab1 is owned and operated by a private individual. The application is used personally and does not provide functionality for third-party users.

2. How Art-Lab1 Works

Art-Lab1 publishes content to the owner’s Pinterest account using OAuth authentication. An access token is created and stored locally on the owner’s device or in environment variables.

3. Data We Process

  • Authorization data: Pinterest OAuth access token, expiration time, and target board ID — stored locally for Art-Lab1 to function.
  • Publication content: images, descriptions, and links intentionally uploaded by the owner through Art-Lab1.
  • Operation logs: optional local logs (error messages, timestamps). These are not shared with third parties.

Art-Lab1 does not collect personal data from third-party users, does not create accounts, and does not perform behavioral analytics.

4. Data Sources

All data is provided by the owner or generated automatically through interaction with the Pinterest API.

5. Purpose and Legal Basis

  • Purpose: authentication with Pinterest and content publishing initiated by the owner through Art-Lab1.
  • Legal basis: the owner’s legitimate interest in processing their own data for personal use of Art-Lab1.

6. Data Disclosure

Data is not shared with third parties except for necessary exchanges with Pinterest for content publication via API. We do not sell or rent data.

7. Storage and Security

  • Tokens and settings are kept locally (e.g., configuration files or environment variables) and should not be published.
  • Access to the device and source code repository should be restricted; consider using a secrets manager.
  • Content uploaded to Pinterest is stored and processed under Pinterest’s policies.

8. Cookies and Trackers

Art-Lab1 itself does not use cookies. When visiting external sites (such as pinterest.com), their respective cookie policies apply.

9. Your Rights

Since Art-Lab1 is used personally and does not handle third-party data, standard data subject rights apply only to the owner’s own account and content within Pinterest.

10. Third-Party Services

Primary third-party service: Pinterest. Use is governed by Pinterest’s Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. Pinterest may receive technical data required to deliver and process content via their API.

11. Retention Period

  • Tokens and configuration: until revoked or deleted by the owner.
  • Local logs: at the owner’s discretion (recommended to delete regularly).
  • Pins on Pinterest: until deleted by the owner within Pinterest’s interface.

12. Revoking Access and Deleting Data

  • Revoke OAuth access in Pinterest developer settings or regenerate/delete the token locally.
  • Delete published pins directly in Pinterest.
  • Remove configuration files and logs from the local device.

13. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. The latest revision date is shown at the top of the page.

This Privacy Policy applies to Art-Lab1.
